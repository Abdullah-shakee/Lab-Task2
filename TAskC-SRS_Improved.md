# TASK C — Corrected Requirements

## CR1 (Replaces R2 & R3)
The system shall allow course registration from the semester start date until the official registration deadline. Late registration shall be allowed for 3 calendar days after the deadline with a late fee.

---

## CR2 (Replaces R4 & R5)
The system shall prevent enrollment when available seats are zero and shall automatically place the student on the waitlist.

---

## CR3 (Replaces R9 & R10)
The system shall prevent students from registering for overlapping courses unless one course is fully asynchronous online.

---

## CR4 (Replaces R11 & R12)
The system shall limit students to 18 credit hours per semester. Students may register for up to 24 credit hours only after advisor approval recorded in the system.

---

## CR5 (Replaces R6)
The system shall send an email confirmation within 2 minutes after successful course registration.

---

## CR6 (Replaces R15)
The system shall not allow course drops after the end of Week 6 of the semester.

---

## CR7 (Improves R8)
The system shall allow prerequisite override only if the instructor submits digital approval through the system.

---

## CR8 (Improves R16–R18)
The system shall enroll waitlisted students automatically in first-in-first-out (FIFO) order when a seat becomes available.

---

## CR9 (Improves R19)
The system shall open registration 48 hours earlier for final-year students before general registration begins.

---

## CR10 (Replaces R20)
The system shall complete course registration transactions within 2 seconds under normal operating conditions.
